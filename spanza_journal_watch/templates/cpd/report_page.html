CPD Activity Report

What is this?

ANZCA requires Fellows and trainees to log reading activities as part of their Continuing Professional Development (CPD) program. SPANZA Journal Watch can track which journal articles you access the full text of and generate a reference list for your CPD submission.

To start tracking, enable CPD tracking in your profile menu. Each time you click a "Full text" link, the article will be recorded. You can then generate a PDF report covering any date range.

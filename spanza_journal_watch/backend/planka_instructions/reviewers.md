## Reviewer workflow

1. Claim a card from **Candidates**.
2. Move it to **Under review** when actively drafting.
3. Write your review in the card **description**, **below** the `< --- Please write your review below this line --- >` marker. Please do not delete this marker
4. Keep the article context section above the marker intact unless correction is needed.
5. Ensure the card is moved to **Publish ready** when handoff is complete.

## Choosing an article

All articles in the **Candidates** column are up for grabs. You may filter these by journal using the 'Labels' button, or search using the 'search cards' function

If an article you would like to cover is not listed - not to worry. You may create your own card for your article; see below for details.

## Adding an article that is not listed

If the article you'd like to review does not exist in the **Candidates** category, you may create a new card and write your review there.

On your new card, please:

- Add yourself as a member (Add to card -> Members)
- Include the details of the article you're reviewing (an article title or URL is sufficient)
- Place the card in **Under review** while writing your draft
- Move the card to **Publish ready** once finished

## Attaching files

If you'd prefer to use another application to write your review (eg. a word document), you may upload this to the card.

Your formatting will not be retained in the upload to the website when attached in this way, but your editor will attempt to reconstruct this as accurately as possible.

## Commenting on others' reviews

You are welcome to collaborate and provide constructive feedback on other people's reviews. Please use the comments section for this

**Please do not edit other people's cards directly**. If your card has been edited and you need this reverted, please let me know directly at [eamonn.upperton@gmail.com](mailto:eamonn.upperton@gmail.com).

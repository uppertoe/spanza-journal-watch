# Welcome to Journal Watch

Welcome to the Journal Watch review platform — we're glad you're here.

## What is this space for?

This platform is where our reviewers collaborate on each journal watch issue. You can use it to:

- **Browse articles** selected for the current issue and pick ones you'd like to review
- **Comment** on articles of interest, even if you're not the assigned reviewer — your perspective is always welcome
- **Submit your completed reviews** for publishing by moving your card to the Publish list

## Your work is backed up

Reviews and uploaded files are stored securely and backed up regularly. That said, we encourage you to keep a personal copy of anything particularly important to you, just in case.

## Getting started

A coordinator will guide you through the process. If you have any questions at any time, use the Comments section on any card or reach out to your regional coordinator directly.
